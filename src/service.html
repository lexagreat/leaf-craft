    Drive More Customers with Local SEO by Lead Craft

    At Lead Craft, we help businesses operating in specific regions strengthen their presence in local search results and attract more nearby customers. Our solutions are especially effective for location-dependent businesses — from retail stores to multi-location brands.

    We take a comprehensive approach to local SEO by leveraging local backlinks, on-page optimization, customer review management, and profile optimization on Google, Apple, and Bing Maps.

    Increase Brand Awareness and Online Presence
    • ✦ Conduct ongoing local keyword research to target location-specific search queries
    • ✦ Optimize web pages and list your business in relevant online directories
    • ✦ Strengthen site authority with high-quality local backlinks
    Improve Local Rankings and Drive More Traffic
    • ✦ Set up and optimize your Google Business Profile
    • ✦ Implement on-page SEO tailored to local keywords
    • ✦ Ensure a mobile-friendly website experience for users on the go
    Boost Conversions and Attract More Local Customers
    • ✦ Create high-performing landing pages with content and CTAs tailored to local audiences
    • ✦ Launch geo-targeted ads on social media platforms
    • ✦ Build trust with customer reviews, case studies, badges, and ratings

    What You Get with Our Local SEO Services

    • Local SEO Keyword Research
      We carefully analyze local search trends and assign the most relevant keywords to your site’s content. This ensures your business appears in front of the right audience by targeting location-specific phrases that drive high-intent, local traffic.
    • Website Optimization & Local Traffic Growth
      Our on-page SEO strategies are designed to bring in nearby customers and improve conversion rates. By enhancing your site’s structure, usability, and mobile performance, we make it easier for local users to navigate your website and take action.
    • Google Business Profile Optimization
      We make sure your Google Business Profile (formerly Google My Business) is properly set up, regularly updated, and optimized with relevant local keywords. This boosts your visibility in map results and helps attract more attention from nearby customers.
    • Customer Review & Testimonial Management
      We help you build trust by encouraging positive reviews and managing customer feedback across platforms. A strong review presence increases credibility and helps bring in more potential customers from your local area.
