    • New (created less than a year ago). The site's operating time is counted not from the moment of address registration, but from the first indexation in the network. Optimization of young sites of this type is the most difficult, since PS practically "do not see" them.
    • Unused sites. If a site is abandoned, its content is not updated, then no matter how successful it is, over time, search engines' trust in such a resource falls, and it goes down in the ranking.
